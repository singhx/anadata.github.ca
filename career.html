What are the next steps ?

So you have submitted your resume what happens next ?

  • 1.  Resume and Cover Letter Submitted

    Thanks for applying! We’ve received your résumé and we’re reading through it. Only successful candidates will be contacted for a phone interview.

  • 2. Phone call from Ana-Data recruitment

    One of our lovely and talented recruiters will contact you to discuss your résumé, test your language and technical skills, and chat about your work experience. This should take around 20 minutes. If there’s a match, we’ll invite you to an in-person interview.

  • 3. Invitation to in-person interview

    Only successful candidates will be invited for in person interview. During this exciting, one-day interviewing event, a number of things will happen:
    A discussion about the benefits and challenges of working at Ana-Data
    A face-to-face interview with one of our recruiters (approx. 25 to 30 mins.)
    A discussion about salary, employee commitment, and working as part of a union

  • 4. Welcome to the team!

    After passing both your safety and customer service training, you’ll officially be a part of the Ana Data Team. A big congrats and welcome!
